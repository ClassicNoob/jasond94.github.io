About

Jason Downing is Junior at the University of Massachusetts, Lowell, where he studies Computer Science. Jason's hobbies include mountain biking, skiing, PC gaming and coding. In the past, Jason has worked at several retail stores, including Sears, Kohl's and Market Basket. He currently works for the Engaging Computer Group at UMass Lowell, and recently finished an internship at Fidelity Investments.

Jason spent Summer 2015 working at Fidelity Investments in Merrimack, NH as a technical intern. He used a variety of Mainframe technologies, such as COBOL, JCL and DB2 / SQL for Fidelity's Brokerage system, which runs on IBM Mainframes. Jason will also be interning again at Fidelity Investments next summer on the mobile development team. He will likely be working on Fidelity's Android applications.
